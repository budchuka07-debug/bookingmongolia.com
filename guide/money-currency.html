Money & Currency in Mongolia

The local currency is Mongolian Tugrik (MNT).

Cash and Card

Cards work in Ulaanbaatar and some tourist areas, but cash is important in rural Mongolia.

ATMs

ATMs are available in cities and larger towns, but can be limited in remote places.

Travel Budget

Independent travel costs vary depending on transport, accommodation and route length.
