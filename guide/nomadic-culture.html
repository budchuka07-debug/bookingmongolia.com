Nomadic Culture in Mongolia

Nomadic Culture in Mongolia

Mongolia is one of the last countries where traditional nomadic life is still alive. For centuries, families have moved seasonally with their livestock across the steppe.

Ger Life

The Mongolian ger is designed for mobility, warmth and practicality. It is central to nomadic family life.

Livestock

Nomads traditionally herd horses, sheep, goats, cattle/yaks and camels.

Hospitality

Guests are often welcomed with milk tea, dairy foods and warm hospitality.
