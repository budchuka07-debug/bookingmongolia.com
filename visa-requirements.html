Mongolia Visa Requirements

A practical travel page for visitors planning a trip to Mongolia. Always confirm your nationality, visa type, and latest requirements on Mongolia’s official immigration and eVisa websites before booking flights.

Official Immigration Site Official eVisa Portal

Do you need a visa?

Mongolia has a mix of visa-free entry, embassy or consulate visa, and eVisa options depending on your nationality and purpose of travel. The safest way is to check your country and trip purpose on the official Immigration Agency website before you buy tickets.

Documents you may need

Exact requirements depend on your nationality, purpose of travel, and where you apply.

Passport
Usually must be valid for at least 6 months and have blank pages.
Visa application form
Required for embassy or consulate applications and some other visa routes.
Recent passport photo
Consular pages commonly require a 3.5 x 4.5 cm photo with a plain background.
Payment proof
Visa fees are generally non-refundable once paid.
Travel proof
Hotel booking or accommodation information, and arrival/departure ticket copies may be requested.
Proof of funds
Recent bank statements may be requested for tourist visas at some missions.
Invitation / approval
Business, work, student, long-stay, or other non-tourist visas can require approval or invitation from the relevant Mongolian authority or inviting entity.

eVisa and application tips

  • Use the official eVisa portal for eligible nationalities and purposes.
  • Prepare a clear passport scan and photo before starting.
  • Double-check passport number, dates, and spelling before payment.
  • Keep a copy of your application confirmation and final visa result.
  • If you are not eligible for eVisa, contact the nearest Mongolian embassy or consulate.

Visa fees and processing

There is no single worldwide visa price for everyone. The exact fee can depend on your nationality, visa type, where you apply, whether you use regular or express processing, and whether you apply online or through a diplomatic mission.

Important: visa fees are generally non-refundable once the transaction is completed or if the visa is refused.

Before paying, always confirm the fee on the official application page or on the website of the Mongolian embassy or consulate where you will apply.

Frequently asked questions

How long should my passport be valid?

As a rule, at least 6 months beyond your intended travel date is commonly required.

Can I apply online?

Yes, if your nationality and travel purpose are eligible for the official eVisa system.

Do I need hotel booking documents?

Often yes for tourism cases. Keep hotel or accommodation details ready.

Can requirements change?

Yes. Always re-check the official site shortly before payment and before departure.

Quick travel checklist

  • Check if your nationality is visa-free
  • Check if you are eligible for eVisa
  • Make sure your passport has 6+ months validity
  • Prepare passport photo and travel documents
  • Confirm fee and processing with the official source
  • Keep digital and printed copies of your visa result
