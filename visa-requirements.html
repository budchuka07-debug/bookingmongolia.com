Mongolia Visa Requirements

Check if you need a visa

Do you need a visa?

Many travelers can enter Mongolia visa-free depending on nationality. Others must apply for a visa before arrival.

Visa Types

  • eVisa (online – fastest)
  • Embassy visa
  • Transit visa

Required Documents

  • Passport (valid 6+ months)
  • Visa application form
  • Passport photo
  • Travel plan / hotel booking
  • Invitation letter (sometimes required)

Visa Fees

Tourist visa fees typically range from $50 – $70 USD. Prices may vary depending on country and processing type.

eVisa (Recommended)

Apply online for Mongolia eVisa for tourism or business travel. Fast and easy process.

Important Policy

  • Visa rules may change anytime
  • Always check official website before travel
  • Keep digital and printed copy of your visa
